My process

1

Discover

We dive deep into your brand, audience, and goals. I conduct stakeholder interviews, market research, and define the creative brief together.

2

Concept

I sketch multiple directions and present 2–3 distinct concepts. We refine the strongest direction based on your feedback and strategy.

3

Iterate

Detailed design development, colour exploration, typography, and revisions. You're involved at every milestone to ensure alignment.

4

Deliver

Final files in all required formats (print, digital, source), plus a style guide and handoff support. Your project is ready to launch.

Frequently asked

What is your typical timeline?
Most projects take 2‑4 weeks from brief to final files, depending on complexity. Branding projects average 3 weeks, while book covers can be delivered in 10 days. I'll provide a clear schedule upfront.
Do you offer revisions?
Yes, I include up to three revision rounds to ensure you're 100% satisfied. Additional revisions can be arranged at a nominal fee. My goal is to get it right, not just done.
What formats will I receive?
You'll get print‑ready files (PDF, AI, EPS) and digital formats (PNG, SVG, Figma). I also provide a style guide and organise all assets in a shared folder for easy access.
